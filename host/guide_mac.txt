Local TCP Bridge - MacOS Setup
-----------------------------------------

1. Double-click the 'install_mac.pkg' file.
2. Follow the professional setup wizard.
3. If macOS blocks it, Right-Click -> Open.
4. IMPORTANT: Restart Chrome (Cmd+Q) after installation.

Need to remove it? Run 'uninstall_mac.sh' to completely remove the bridge from your system.

-----------------------------------------
🗑️ UNINSTALLATION
-----------------------------------------
Should you need to remove the bridge from your Mac:
1. Open a terminal.
2. Run: bash uninstall_mac.sh
3. This will remove the files and the Chrome registration.
